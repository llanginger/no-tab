Tabulatr

A plugin for better tab management

Tabulatr is an extension that helps to make your browsing experience more efficient by preventing you from opening multiple tabs of websites you only ever need one of open at a time. If you open a tracked site in two separate tabs, Tabulatr will automatically bring the first tab into view and close the second tab, meaning you will never end the day with a bunch of minimized facebook tabs!

To add a site to Tabulatr, navigate to the site you want Tabulatr to work on, copy the URL, paste it into the field below and hit "Add New Site". Please feel free to submit suggestions for sites I should add to the included list here!

For the extension to work properly, please make sure you close duplicate tabs of any site you want Tabluatr to work on - this will ensure that you get the correct behavior.
